Your System is a Language

Jeff Eaton presented “Your System is a Language” at UX Camp Winter 2021. Enjoy!

Jeff Eaton

Partner, Autogram

Jeff Eaton helps large organizations understand, model, and manage their content. For 25 years, he’s tackled digital publishing, web development, and content strategy problems for organizations large and small.

Whether he’s fixing problems with CMS architecture or editorial workflow, his work sits in the overlap between design, communications, and technology. Today, he’s a partner at Autogram, a strategic consultancy focused on the intersection of design systems and high-variance content.
